MONSTERS | Metal Band

Throughout my entire life, music has been a passion of mine. I've been in plenty of bands from pop-punk to death metal, but my professional endeavor into music came with my band, MONSTERS. As a "deathcore" band from Chicago which I co-founded in 2008, we toured for 2+ years across the entire US and Canada, put out two albums and were represented by an indie label, management company, and a booking agency.


I'm really proud of my work here from writing and recording most of our music, to running the bands logistics and merchandising, and of course performing live every night on guitar. I had a major part in all aspects of running a successful band.

Depending on who you are, the music PROBABLY is not for you...but I encourage you to check out some tracks below. Enjoy!
